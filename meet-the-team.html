Oliver Wong

Director - LA, NY

Oliver Wong is a Taiwanese screenwriter, director, actor, and stand-up comedian. With the given name of Chia-Yao Wang, Oliver Wong is born and raised in Taiwan on March 4th, 1992. He majored in Radio and Television at National Chengchi University in Taiwan, where he explored and discovered his artistic voice by directing and writing short films. During the final year of his college, he directed, wrote, and edited his thesis film, 100 Apples. A telling of a high schooler struggling to accept his homosexuality, 100 Apples gained international recognition in numerous film festivals. Oliver has created an established career as Director and often draws comedy inspiration from his lost-in-translation situations, Asian-American experiences, and American gay culture.
