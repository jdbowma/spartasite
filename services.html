About Us

Sparta Productions is a Los Angeles-based media company founded and led by Roberta Sparta. With operations covering all corners of the country, Sparta continues to build upon and adhere to its principles of authenticity, collaboration, and excellence. Having worked as an actor, producer, line producer, assistant director, and director, among other positions in the film industry, Roberta brings to set a profound understanding of all departments of production. Critical scheduling skills, industry-bred coordination and management abilities, and a comprehensive knowledge of the business of filmmaking are the foundation of Roberta's expansive portfolio of film and vertical content.
